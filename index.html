What We Offer

Our Gym & Fitness
Cleaning Services

Whether you run a large commercial gym or a boutique fitness studio, our experienced cleaning crew delivers consistent, high-quality results that keep your members safe and coming back.

Keep your fitness facility consistently clean with our scheduled gym cleaning services. We offer daily, weekly, and bi-weekly cleaning plans tailored to your gym's hours and traffic. Our professional gym cleaners handle everything from floor mopping and equipment wiping to restroom sanitation and front desk upkeep — so your facility always makes the right first impression.
High-touch gym equipment is a breeding ground for germs and bacteria. Our equipment disinfecting service thoroughly sanitizes treadmills, weight machines, free weights, cables, benches, and cardio equipment using EPA-approved, gym-safe disinfectants. We help you maintain a hygienic environment your members can trust — and a reputation that sets you apart.
Locker rooms and showers demand a higher standard of cleanliness. Our gym janitorial team deep-cleans and disinfects showers, toilet areas, sinks, benches, and lockers to prevent mold, mildew, and odor. We restock supplies, scrub tile grout, and ensure your locker room leaves members with a positive, lasting impression.
Gym floors and mats take a beating every day. Our flooring deep clean service tackles rubber flooring, yoga mats, wrestling mats, turf, and hardwood — removing sweat, dirt, and bacteria from the surface and deep within the material. Perfect for yoga studios, pilates studios, CrossFit boxes, and boutique fitness studios that demand pristine floors.
Hosting a fitness competition, open house, or special event? Our post-event deep cleaning crew restores your facility to peak condition fast. We also offer seasonal deep cleans that go beyond your regular routine — targeting vents, baseboards, high surfaces, and all the spots that daily cleaning misses. Eco-friendly, EPA-approved products at no extra cost.
